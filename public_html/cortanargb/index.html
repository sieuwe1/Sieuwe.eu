What is CortanaRGB?

CortanaRGB is worlds most affordable Cortana activated RGB ledstrip. With CortanaRGB you will be able to control a RGB ledstrip using your voice. Weather you want to: lighten your room, Show off your gaming PC or have a disco light, CortanaRGB can do it! Because CortanaRGB intergrates Cortana it has excellent voice recogonition and also seamlessly intergrates with Windows 10 using the CortanaRGB app.
